Community Resilience and Adaptation Funding Tool (CRAFT)

EMCR provides funding programs that offer support to First Nations and Local Governments in B.C. to enhance their capacity to withstand and adapt to natural and climate-driven hazards.

Learn More About Disaster Risk Reduction Funding programs

Disaster Resilience and Innovation Funding (DRIF) program

Proponents can submit proposals, manage submissions and report progress on funded projects.

Want more information about the DRIF program? See the DRIF program information

Use this portal if you are applying for DRIF program and representing a:

  • Local Government

Collection Notice:

Your personal information may be collected for the purposes of evaluating applications relating to, reporting on, and administering the Disaster Resilience and Innovation Funding program in accordance with sections 26(c)(e)(g) of the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act. If you have any questions relating to the collection of your personal information, please connect with the Director, Disaster Mitigation and Climate Adaptation at EMCR.DisasterMitigation@gov.bc.ca.

Additional Disaster Risk Reduction Funding programs

There are other Disaster Risk Reduction Funding programs that are not available in CRAFT.

Want more information about other funding? See additional funding program information

Use this portal if you are applying for the DRIF program and are representing a:

  • Wildfire-related projects (e.g. FireSmart Community Funding and Supports) through the Community Resiliency Investment (CRI) program
  • Preparedness-related projects (e.g.: Emergency Operation Centres Equipment and Training, and Emergency Support Services Equipment and Training) continue to be funded through the Community Emergency Preparedness Fund
